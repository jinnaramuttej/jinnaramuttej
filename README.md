## 🧠 Cognify — *AI Startup · In Active Development*

> *This is not a project. It's a product I'm building as a startup.*

**Cognify** is an AI-powered competitive exam preparation platform for **JEE Main, JEE Advanced, NEET, and BITSAT** — the exams that determine the future of millions of Indian students. The platform combines adaptive testing, deep performance analytics, and a conversational AI tutor to make exam prep smarter, not just harder.

### What makes it different

Most exam platforms dump questions at students. Cognify closes the feedback loop:

```
Learn → Practice → Analyze Mistakes → AI Tutor Fixes Gaps → Repeat
```

Every test result answers one question: **"What went wrong, and how do I fix it?"**

### Core Features

**🤖 Cogni — AI Tutor** · Built on Groq + Gemini APIs. Uses the Socratic method — asks guiding questions instead of giving answers, so students actually understand concepts, not just memorize them.

**📊 Adaptive Tests** · The system tracks accuracy, speed, and consistency per chapter. When it detects weak areas (e.g. low accuracy in Thermodynamics), it generates a targeted test automatically.

**🎯 Full Exam Simulation** · Mock tests that feel identical to the real exam — real timer, negative marking, subject sectioning, PYQ-based questions, exam-specific marking schemes for JEE/NEET/BITSAT.

**📈 Deep Analytics** · Tracks not just scores but *patterns* — weak chapters, time management issues, careless vs. conceptual mistakes — and delivers actionable recommendations.

**👥 Multi-role System** · Students, teachers (can assign tests), and admins — full role-based access with Supabase auth.

### Architecture

Fully documented production codebase: feature contracts, module boundaries, DB schema, AI workflow rules, deployment guides. Built to scale from day one.

`Next.js 15 (App Router)` `TypeScript` `Supabase` `Groq API` `Gemini API` `shadcn/ui` `Docker` `Caddy`
